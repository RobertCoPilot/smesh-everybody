# Product Context

## Product
Smesh Everybody is a mobile-first padel match companion for casual clubs, friend groups, and tournament organizers.

## Users
Players who want quick scoring, team setup, match history, rankings, and tournament previews without spreadsheet overhead.

## Register
product

## Design ambition
The app should feel like a sports lineup tool, not a generic SaaS dashboard. It can borrow from collectible sports cards, bracket sheets, and court-side scoreboards, but it should avoid obvious AI UI tropes: purple gradients, generic glass cards, nested cards, and decorative gradient text.

## Themes
- Clay League: warm, sunlit, direct, sharp, clay-court energy.
- Electric League: brighter, rounded, high-energy blue/pink product-card feel.

## UX principles
- One-handed mobile use first.
- Match state must be visible at a glance.
- Touch targets should feel confident and game-like.
- Keep scoring actions explicit and hard to mis-tap.
- Use motion sparingly for result feedback and state changes.
